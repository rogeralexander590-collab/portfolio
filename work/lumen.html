Section01
The problem

A bank built for forms,
not for people.

Lumen's growth had stalled. A polished app store listing was bringing in plenty of downloads, but 73% of users never completed signup.

The onboarding asked for 14 fields across 8 screens before showing a single product feature. The dashboard buried the most-used action three taps deep. Users churned silently.

The team knew something was wrong. They didn't know where.

→ Pain points found
01
15-minute onboarding

Eight separate screens of identity verification before users could see any value.

02
Buried primary action

Sending money — the #1 task — required three taps from the dashboard.

03
No emotional payoff

The app felt like a spreadsheet. Zero moments of delight to anchor retention.

Section06
The solution

A bank that feels good
to open.

We collapsed 8 onboarding screens into 3. Surfaced the primary action. Added warmth where there was once only data. The result is an app that respects time and rewards attention.
